PANEL FENCE

Panel fence designed for those locations to isolate motorways, high flow intersections from pedestrian zone, provides a strong barrier for security and privacy to separate outdoor spaces for homes, worksite and industry areas. It is also used in military areas, premises, border areas, warehouse areas and airports. Panel fencing is the most popular type used for boundaries where aesthetic appeal and integrity of access share equal importance. The simple but effective method of installation provides flexibility to suit a range of requirements and surroundings.

  • Wire diameter: Ø 4.50 mm or Ø 5.00 mm
  • Wire Mesh: 50 × 50 mm or 50 × 150 mm
  • Panel fence wire height: from 55 cm to 250 cm

  • High quality galvanized structure steel
  • PVC coating and Electrostatic painted
  • Contains all assembly and accessories
  • Rust-proofing
  • Durability of a steel fence
  • Low cost
  • Flexible
  • Light weight

  • Optimal packaging during transport
